Our Work for Children in Kenya

Donate4Future is among the lead agencies working with Kenyaâ€™s Humanitarian Partners Team at the national level to identify, treat and prevent hunger and malnutrition.

Our teams are training health workers to identify and treat children for malnutrition and are providing infant- and child-feeding programs to mothers.

We have rolled out screenings for malnutrition, reaching over 19,000 children five years old and younger, and more than 10,000 pregnant women.

In addition, we are helping keep schools and health facilities open by trucking in clean water and providing technical assistance in water, well and storage systems.

And we are helping communities prepare for and prevent future crises by providing technical assistance to develop and implement response plans.

The Challenges for Children in Kenya

Living in poverty and facing extreme hunger and a devastating food crisis, children in Kenya need your help.

36% of people live in poverty

1 child in 22 dies before their 5th birthday - 7 times that of the United States

21% of school-age children are out of school

26% of children suffer from stunting due to malnutrition

12% of girls age 15-19 are married

1 in 12 girls between age 15-19 gives birth

25% of girls age 15 and older struggle to read or write
